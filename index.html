Welcome to AUS Smash

An ONLINE and OFFLINE community dedicated to playing the games that they love

What is AUS SMASH about

Online Communities

Join Discord and Facebook to interact with with other players and learn more about the local scenes around you. Play other games, enjoy online tournaments and other online interactions.

Offline Communities

Come and compete in local tournaments and meets to play Smash Brothers games, everyone is welcome and tournaments are run often no matter where you live!

Netplay

Play online games with friends through Wii U's and Dolphins netplay on Discord. Other games such as Rivals Of Aether are also played competitivly, play to just have fun or to get better at the game.

Online Communities

Connect online with other smashers, to chat or to find out more about your local scene

Discord

Discord is a 21st century chat program with voice and text chat, which has fostered a large community of smashers as well as other games such as Rivals of Aether. Join today for individual state channels and a vibrant netplay community
